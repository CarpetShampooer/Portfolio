About Me:

I began creating art as a hobby in 2005, then graduated with a degree in Game Development with a focus on 2D & 3D Art and Animation. Since then, I've had the opportunity to assist in the development of several professional and independent titles over several platforms including Wii, 3DS, XBOX360, iPhone, Android, and PC. I truly enjoy collaborating with others and learning new tools, techniques, and facets of the design and development process!
